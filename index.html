Instructions:
  1. Fill out Player Names and pick a unique color.
  2. Press START Game button to begin the game.
  3. The STATUS bar will instruct players who's turn it is and if the move is legal.
  4. Pegs move clockwise on the board. Just press the peg you wish to move!
  5. Press the SPIN Button to roll the dice.
  6. A Player must roll a 6 in order to move a peg from HOME to START.
  7. Rolling a 6 allows the player to spin and move again
  8. If your peg lands on a space already occupied by an opponent, the opponent's peg returns to its HOME position and your peg occupies the space.
  9. A peg must rotate once around the field of play. Upon rotating once, a player can then enter the BASE and be free from other opponents.
  10. A player wins when all 4 pegs are safely placed into the BASE
